## EMPIRE DETECTION FRAMEWORK

### THE 4 FORMATION SIGNALS

**1. STRUCTURAL CATALYST**
- New policy/law (nuclear subsidies, defense spending)
- New technology (ChatGPT, blockchain)
- New geopolitical reality (war, sanctions)
- **Requirement:** Multi-year, not temporary

**2. FORCED BUYERS**
- Buyers who MUST purchase regardless of price
- No alternatives available
- Time pressure (farmers, utilities, governments)
- **Requirement:** Structural, not discretionary

**3. SUPPLY CONSTRAINT**
- Physical bottleneck (mines, plants, infrastructure)
- Can't be solved quickly (2+ years minimum)
- Irreplaceable (no substitutes)
- **Requirement:** Structural shortage, not temporary

**4. NARRATIVE <20% ADOPTION**
- Specialists aware, mainstream unaware
- No media coverage (CNBC not talking about it)
- Your friends never heard of it
- **Requirement:** Early stage, pre-hype

**ALL 4 SIGNALS = FORMING EMPIRE → MAXIMUM CONVICTION ACCUMULATION**

---

### THE 4 PEAK SIGNALS

**1. NARRATIVE >80% ADOPTION**
- Everyone talking about it (barber, Uber driver)
- Mainstream media saturation
- Retail FOMO extreme (Reddit front page)

**2. EUPHORIA INDICATORS**
- Absurd valuations (P/E 50+ for cyclicals)
- "This time is different" narratives
- No bears left (100% bullish sentiment)

**3. PARABOLIC PRICE ACTION**
- Vertical moves (10%+ daily)
- Blow-off top volume
- Stocks doubling in weeks

**4. FORCED BUYERS EXHAUSTED**
- Government programs completed
- Institutional allocation maxed
- No new capital entering

**ALL 4 SIGNALS = PEAKING EMPIRE → IMMEDIATE EXIT**
