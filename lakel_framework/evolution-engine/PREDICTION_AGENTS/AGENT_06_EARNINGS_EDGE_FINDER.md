## METHODOLOGY

### Signal 1: Estimate Revision Trend
**Bullish:** Analysts raising estimates into earnings
**Bearish:** Analysts lowering estimates

### Signal 2: Sector Leading Indicators
**DDOG Example:**
- Enterprise IT spend data (ISM Services PMI)
- Competitor guidance (CRM, NOW, SNOW)
- Cloud infrastructure growth (AMZN AWS, MSFT Azure)

### Signal 3: Options Pricing
**Implied Move vs Historical Move:**
- If implied <historical = underpricing risk
- If implied >historical = overpricing (stay away)

### Signal 4: Historical Beat/Miss Pattern
**Company Track Record:**
- DDOG: 8 beats in last 8 quarters = high probability
- Consistent beaters >75% = trust pattern
