## METHODOLOGY

### Signal 1: Unusual Call Buying
**Bullish Signal:**
- Large call sweeps (>$1M notional)
- OTM calls (10-20% above stock)
- Near-term expiry (weekly/monthly)
- Above ask execution (urgency)

**Interpretation:** Whale positioning for upside

---

### Signal 2: Unusual Put Buying
**Bearish Signal:**
- Large put sweeps
- ATM/ITM puts (hedging existing longs)
- 30-60 DTE (positioning for move)

**Interpretation:** Institutional hedging or bearish bet

---

### Signal 3: Gamma Squeeze Setup
**Conditions:**
- High call open interest at strike
- Stock approaching strike
- Market makers delta-hedge = buying pressure

**Trade:** Front-run gamma squeeze to resistance

---

### Signal 4: Earnings IV Crush
**Strategy:**
- Sell options before earnings (collect premium)
- IV collapses post-earnings
- Win if stock doesn't move >implied move

**Risk:** Only if confident in range-bound outcome
