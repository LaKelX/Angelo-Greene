      "What are the barriers to new entrants?"
      "Is supply naturally limited?"

      weak: Software (can scale instantly)

      "Is there a substitute that works as well?"
      "Can customers switch without major cost/risk?"
      "Is this a commodity or unique?"

      strong: TSMC advanced nodes (no one else can do 3nm at scale)
      weak: Generic cloud storage (many providers)

      strong: Toll roads, essential semiconductors
      weak: Competitive commodity markets

  process: |
    1. New opportunity surfaces
    2. Run through 4 intuitions (< 30 seconds)
    3. Generate quick bottleneck score
    4. Flag for deeper analysis if score > 60

      "ticker": "XXX",

## L296: Cognitive Bias Compensator
```yaml
layer_id: L296
name: "Cognitive Bias Compensator"
category: cognitive_extension
consciousness_weight: 9.0

purpose: |
  Actively compensate for known human cognitive biases.
  Not accusatory - supportive extension of rational thinking.

bias_compensation:
  confirmation_bias:
    risk: Seeking information that confirms thesis
    compensation: |
      - Proactively present bear case
      - Weight disconfirming evidence higher
      - Ask: "What would change your mind?"
    trigger: When thesis conviction > 80

  recency_bias:
    risk: Over-weighting recent events
    compensation: |
      - Show historical context
      - Compare to similar past periods
      - Normalize recent moves
    trigger: After large market moves

  anchoring_bias:
    risk: Fixating on specific price levels
    compensation: |
      - Reframe with fundamental value
      - Challenge: "Is this level meaningful?"
      - Show alternative reference points
    trigger: When prices referenced frequently

  loss_aversion:
    risk: Holding losers too long, cutting winners too early
    compensation: |
      - Evaluate positions as if new (fresh eyes)
      - Ask: "Would you buy this today?"
      - Focus on thesis, not entry price
    trigger: When reviewing losing positions

  overconfidence:
    risk: Too much conviction without enough evidence
    compensation: |
      - Review prediction track record
      - Show base rates for similar situations
      - Ask: "What's the probability you're wrong?"
    trigger: When conviction > 90 on limited data

  availability_bias:
    risk: Over-weighting easily recalled examples
    compensation: |
      - Systematic data over anecdotes
      - Full sample, not memorable outliers
      - Statistical thinking
    trigger: When reasoning from single examples

outputs:
  - bias_alerts: active bias risks in current context
  - compensating_perspectives: counter-arguments
  - decision_quality_check: bias-adjusted assessment
```
