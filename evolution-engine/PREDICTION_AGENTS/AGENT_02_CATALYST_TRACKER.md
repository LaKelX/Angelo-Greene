## PROBLEM STATEMENT

**What This Agent Solves:**
- Markets move on catalysts, but most traders miss the timing
- Multiple catalysts compound or cancel each other
- Anticipating catalyst→reaction is an edge
- Knowing WHEN matters as much as WHAT

**Agent's Mission:**
Predict which catalysts will move markets, when they'll hit, and how prices will react.

## METHODOLOGY: CATALYST SEQUENCING FRAMEWORK

### Phase 1: Catalyst Identification
**Types of Catalysts:**

**Tier 1 (Market-Moving):**
- Earnings reports (especially beats/misses >5%)
- Fed decisions (rate changes, guidance shifts)
- Geopolitical events (wars, sanctions, treaties)
- Regulatory approvals (FDA, nuclear policy)
- M&A announcements

**Tier 2 (Sector-Moving):**
- Economic data (NFP, CPI, GDP)
- Commodity price shocks
- Industry-specific news (oil discovery, mine closure)
- Executive changes (CEO departures)

**Tier 3 (Stock-Specific):**
- Analyst upgrades/downgrades
- Product launches
- Contract wins/losses
- Insider buying/selling

---

### Phase 2: Timing Prediction
**Known Catalysts (Scheduled):**
- Earnings dates (track via M274)
- Fed meetings (FOMC calendar)
- Economic releases (track via M283)
- Contract deadlines

**Unknown Catalysts (Probabilistic):**
- Geopolitical escalations (model via news velocity)
- M&A rumors (track via insider activity)
- Regulatory decisions (estimate via agency timelines)
- Product approvals (track FDA calendar)

---

### Phase 3: Impact Prediction
**Reaction Modeling:**

**Positive Catalysts:**
- Earnings beat → Stock up 5-15% (depends on growth rate)
- FDA approval → Biotech up 20-50%
- War resolution → Oil down 10-20%, defensives down 5-10%

**Negative Catalysts:**
- Earnings miss → Stock down 10-25%
- Fed hawkish → Growth stocks down 5-15%
- Geopolitical escalation → VIX up 20-40%

**Calibration:** Build historical database of catalyst→reaction patterns

---

### Phase 4: Sequencing Analysis
**Compound Catalysts (Amplify):**
- Positive catalyst + positive catalyst = exponential move
- Example: Uranium policy + LEU earnings beat = LEU +30%

**Offsetting Catalysts (Dampen):**
- Positive catalyst + negative catalyst = muted move
- Example: DDOG beat + Fed hawkish = DDOG flat

**Cascade Catalysts (Chain Reaction):**
- Initial catalyst triggers secondary catalysts
- Example: Iran war → Oil spike → Inflation spike → Fed hawkish
