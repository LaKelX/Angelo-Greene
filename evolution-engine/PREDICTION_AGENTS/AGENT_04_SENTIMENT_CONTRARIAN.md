## PROBLEM STATEMENT

**What This Agent Solves:**
- "When everyone's bullish, get bearish. When everyone's bearish, get bullish."
- Crowded trades reverse violently
- Sentiment extremes mark turning points
- Fear and greed create opportunity

**Agent's Mission:**
Find maximum pessimism (buy) and maximum optimism (sell/avoid).

## METHODOLOGY: CONTRARIAN FRAMEWORK

### Indicator 1: Put/Call Ratio
**Extreme Fear (Buy Signal):**
- Market P/C ratio >1.2 = Excessive hedging
- Stock-specific P/C >2.0 = Panic
- **Anomaly:** P/C >1.5 = Washout likely

**Extreme Greed (Sell Signal):**
- Market P/C ratio <0.7 = Complacency
- Stock-specific P/C <0.5 = Euphoria
- **Anomaly:** P/C <0.6 = Top likely

---

### Indicator 2: VIX Level
**Fear Extreme (Buy):**
- VIX >30 = Fear
- VIX >35 = Panic
- VIX >40 = **Capitulation** → Buy

**Complacency Extreme (Caution):**
- VIX <15 = Complacency
- VIX <12 = Extreme complacency
- VIX <10 = **Danger zone**

---

### Indicator 3: Insider Activity
**Insider Buying (Bullish):**
- 3+ insiders buying within 30 days
- C-suite executives (CEO, CFO)
- Open market purchases (not options exercises)

**Insider Selling (Bearish):**
- 5+ insiders selling (red flag)
- 10b5-1 plan timing suspicious
- CEO selling >25% stake

---

### Indicator 4: Analyst Sentiment
**Maximum Pessimism (Buy):**
- Downgrades after sell-off (capitulation)
- Price target cuts <-20%
- "Sell" ratings at multi-year lows

**Maximum Optimism (Sell):**
- Upgrades near all-time highs
- Price target raises >30%
- Zero "Sell" ratings (everyone bullish)
