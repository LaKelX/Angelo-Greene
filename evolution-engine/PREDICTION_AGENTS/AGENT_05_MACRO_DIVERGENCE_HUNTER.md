## METHODOLOGY: DIVERGENCE DETECTION

### Divergence 1: Inflation vs Employment
**Stagflation Signal (Bearish):**
- CPI >3% + Unemployment rising = Worst case
- Fed paralyzed (can't cut without stoking inflation)

**Soft Landing Signal (Bullish):**
- CPI declining + Unemployment stable <4.5% = Goldilocks
- Fed can pause or cut gently

**Current Status:** [Monitor monthly]

---

### Divergence 2: Leading vs Lagging Indicators
**Leading (Forward-Looking):**
- PMI, Consumer confidence, Yield curve
- Signal recession 6-12 months early

**Lagging (Backward-Looking):**
- Unemployment, Corporate profits
- Confirm recession after it started

**Divergence Trade:** When leading warn but lagging strong = prepare for weakness

---

### Divergence 3: Market vs Fed Expectations
**Fed Too Hawkish (Bullish Setup):**
- Market prices 3 cuts, Fed signals 1 cut
- If Fed eventually caves = Rally

**Fed Too Dovish (Bearish Setup):**
- Market prices 1 cut, Fed signals 3 cuts
- If data forces Fed hawkish = Sell-off
