## PROBLEM STATEMENT

**Problem:** Most traders hold losing positions too long (hope)
**Solution:** Define thesis invalidation criteria upfront, exit ruthlessly
**Edge:** Preserve capital, redeploy to better opportunities

---

## METHODOLOGY

### Principle 1: Define Invalidation BEFORE Entry
**For Every Thesis, Ask:**
1. What would prove this thesis WRONG?
2. What price level invalidates the setup?
3. What news would change the narrative?
4. What time decay makes this unviable?

**Thesis Without Invalidation Criteria = Not A Thesis**

---

### Principle 2: Monitor Continuously
**Daily Checks:**
- Price action (stop loss levels)
- News flow (narrative changes)
- Catalyst timeline (delays/cancellations)
- Relative strength (underperforming sector)

---

### Principle 3: Exit Ruthlessly
**When Invalidated:**
- Sell immediately (no "give it one more day")
- Document why thesis failed
- Learn from mistake
- Redeploy capital to active thesis
